Sausage Pasta

Description

Spicy sausage pasta is a delicious, simple, and quick recipe that you can whip up in no time. It's perfect for a weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Spicy sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Sauce of your choice
  • Heavy cream
  • Spinach
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Red pepper
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oregano
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and add the sausage, breaking it up with a spoon.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, and red pepper flakes. Cook until the sausage is browned and the onion is soft.
  4. Add the sauce, heavy cream, and oregano. Simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Add the spinach and cook until wilted.
  6. Stir in the cooked pasta and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Season with seasonings to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
